# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: October 1, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Euno ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Discord bot and associated services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information from Discord

- **User Information**: Discord user ID, username, discriminator
- **Server Information**: Discord server (guild) ID, server name, member count
- **Channel Information**: Channel IDs and names where the bot is used
- **Message Data**: Message content, timestamps, author IDs (for moderation purposes)
- **Member Information**: Role assignments, join dates, permissions

### 2.2 Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address (via Discord OAuth)
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store credit card details)
- **Configuration Data**: Bot settings, moderation rules, channel configurations

### 2.3 Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: Commands executed, features used, interaction timestamps
- **Technical Data**: IP addresses (via server logs), browser information, device type
- **Analytics Data**: Aggregated usage statistics, performance metrics

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

### 3.1 Provide and Maintain the Service

- Process moderation actions in your Discord server
- Track and analyze message statistics
- Maintain server configurations and settings
- Provide customer support

### 3.2 Improve the Service

- Analyze usage patterns and feature adoption
- Debug technical issues and improve performance
- Develop new features based on user needs
- Conduct internal research and analytics

### 3.3 Communicate with You

- Send service-related notifications
- Respond to inquiries and support requests
- Send billing and subscription information
- Notify about changes to terms or features (if you opt in)

### 3.4 Security and Compliance

- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect rights and safety of users

## 4. Data Retention

### 4.1 Active Data

- **Message Data**: Retained while your server uses the Service, with automatic cleanup after 90 days for free tier, unlimited for Pro tier
- **User Data**: Retained while you have an active account
- **Analytics Data**: Retained for up to 2 years

### 4.2 Deleted Data

- When you remove the bot from your server, data is marked for deletion within 30 days
- When you delete your account, personal data is deleted within 30 days
- Aggregated anonymous analytics may be retained indefinitely

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### 5.2 Service Providers

We share data with trusted service providers who help us operate:

- **Stripe**: Payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
- **Sentry**: Error tracking and monitoring
- **DigitalOcean**: Cloud hosting infrastructure
- **Amplitude**: Analytics (if configured)

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

### 5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or in response to:

- Court orders or subpoenas
- Legal processes or government requests
- Protection of rights, property, or safety
- Investigation of potential Terms of Service violations

### 5.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Request a copy of your data in a portable format
- Review what information we have collected

### 6.2 Correction and Deletion

You can:

- Correct inaccurate information through your account settings
- Request deletion of your personal data
- Remove the bot from your server to stop data collection

### 6.3 Opt-Out Rights

You may opt out of:

- Marketing communications (we don't send these by default)

### 6.4 GDPR Rights (EU/UK Users)

If you are in the EU or UK, you have additional rights:

- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@euno.reactiflux.com

### 6.5 CCPA Rights (California Users)

California residents have the right to:

- Know what personal information is collected
- Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
- Request deletion of personal information
- Not be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights
